About us

Globalize is helping companies around the world to expand to different markets. We have experts for different countries, which can help you with every aspect of entering foreign markets from the language barier to executuion. We are a company aimed at helping mainly SME’s, but also large companies, to reach global markets without budgets of global companies. We are oriented at growing customer base, nurturing them and turning them into a profitable assets. Everything with a professional, but human touch.

Globalization Process

See our standard project approximation. Of course, the process differs from project to project, but it is usually similiar to this example.

START

Right now

We analyze your market and prepare a detailed outline of how could you grow your business by expansion to numerous countries.

Planning

1 Week

Together, we elaborate the most suitable cooperation plan and choose the most efficient services based on your needs.

Setup

2 - 3 Weeks

We start setting basis for your services and assign you an account manager. This person will guide you through the setup process, program your account, work with internal operations and training departments to ensure efficient implementation, and facilitate the test service period.

Team Allocation

1 Month

We establish a dedicated team for you that is experienced and well-versed in your business niche. Backed by a large experience a scenario-based testing, they’ll easily integrate into your business processes.

Go Live!

2 Months

After a successful testing period we go live and keep an eye on your growth goals. We look forward to a long term and fruitful cooperation!
